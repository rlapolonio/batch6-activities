Let's play PIG GAME!
  • The game consists of 2 players, taking turns per round.
  • Each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as the player wishes. Each result is added to the player's ROUND score.
  • However, if the player rolls a 1, all of the player's ROUND score gets lost and the player's turn ends.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', adding the player's current ROUND score to the player's GLOBAL score, ending the turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
